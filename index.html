Privacy Policy for Eventify

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Eventify is designed to help users save, organize, and manage event posts and links from Instagram and other platforms. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how Eventify handles your data.

Information We Collect

Eventify does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The app stores ONLY the data you manually enter or import (such as event titles, dates, notes, links, and captions). This data is stored locally on your device only and never uploaded to any server.

Instagram Data

Eventify allows users to paste public Instagram post links to extract captions and event information. Eventify:

  • Does NOT log Instagram credentials
  • Does NOT access your Instagram account
  • Does NOT store any Instagram data outside your device

The extraction is performed locally using a WebView within your device.

Local Storage Only

All event details, saved captions, and downloaded images remain securely stored on your device. If you uninstall the app, all stored data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

Eventify does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

Your Control

You fully control all data inside Eventify. You can edit or delete your saved events at any time from within the app.

Children’s Privacy

Eventify does not target children under 13, and it does not collect personal data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
